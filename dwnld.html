Common Software Issues

As of yet, the most prominent issue with our software is its deletion upon being detected by Anti-virus programs. We assure you that this is a false positive.

Due to the nature of our program being a server hosting and monitoring program, antiviruses see it as a potential threat.

To remedy the issue, please whitelist the program on your antivirus if there is an option, or simply turn off your anti-virus while using the program. You may even use the program on a virtual OS if networking is enabled.
